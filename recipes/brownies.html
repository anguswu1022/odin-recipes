Brownies Recipe

Description

The best brownies are those that are moist and fudgy on the inside while the outside has a crunchy texture. They're a classic snack that can be eaten at any time of day. Not to mention, these should only take 1 hour to bake!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sifted unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sifted powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 tsp. sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp. water
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 Fahrenheit and lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Line the baking dish with parchment paper and spray the parchment paper.
  3. Combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, powdered sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Whisk together the eggs, oil, water, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
  5. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until the mixtures are fully incorporated.
  6. Pour the batter into the baking dish and smooth out the top with a spatula.
  7. Bake the mix for 40 to 48 minutes.
  8. Let the brownies cool down before slicing and serve.
